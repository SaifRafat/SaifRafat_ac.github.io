Hello! I am M. Saifuzzaman Rafat, an aspiring AI researcher with a deep interest in cutting-edge technologies such as computer vision, machine learning, and generative AI. I am currently pursuing my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Rajshahi, Bangladesh. I am also working as a Research Assistant at the Center for Research and Innovation (CRI) Lab at the University of Rajshahi, where I contribute to various research projects focused on machine learning and AI applications.

## Research Interest

My primary research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and generative AI. I am particularly passionate about developing interdisciplinary solutions that have a real impact on society. Throughout my academic journey, I have focused on leveraging deep learning techniques for practical applications, aiming to create industry-grade technologies that are both impactful and widely applicable. My goal is to utilize generative AI to address real-world challenges and contribute to societal good.
